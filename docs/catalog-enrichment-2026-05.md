## 2026 Specialty Coffee Trends

**Processing methods gaining popularity:**
- Anaerobic fermentation and co-fermented lots (especially from Colombia) continue to dominate competition circuits and high-score lists.
- Carbonic maceration and thermal shock treatments are producing unusual flavor profiles and consistently scoring 90+ at Cup of Excellence.
- Honey and natural processing remain popular for fruit-forward profiles.

**Emerging origins:**
- Asian origins are rising — Thailand, Myanmar, China, and India are showing improved quality and growing consumer interest.
- Ecuador is appearing more frequently on specialty menus (e.g., Sey's current La Capilla lot).

**Other trends:**
- Filter coffee gaining ground in Europe, shifting from traditional espresso dominance.
- Single-origin subscriptions and rotating seasonal menus are now standard at top roasters.
- Sustainability focus deepening — compostable packaging (Driftaway), producer-set pricing (April), and direct-trade relationships remain key differentiators.
- Functional coffee beverages and specialty instant/capsule formats expanding.
